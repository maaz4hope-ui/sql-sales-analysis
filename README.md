# 📊 SQL Sales Analysis Project

## 🔍 Overview
Analyzed a retail sales dataset using SQL to uncover
key business insights across products, categories,
cities, and months.

---

## 💡 Business Questions & Insights

### 1. 🏆 Which category generates most revenue?
**Electronics** dominates with **Rs. 353,000**
vs Furniture at Rs. 74,000

### 2. 🌆 Which city has highest sales?
**Lahore** leads with **Rs. 175,000**
followed by Karachi (Rs. 155,000) & Islamabad (Rs. 97,000)

### 3. 📅 Which month had peak sales?
**January** was the best month — **Rs. 130,000** 🔥
April was the slowest at Rs. 20,000 📉
> 💼 Recommendation: Increase marketing budget in April

### 4. 🛒 Top selling products (above Rs. 50,000)?
| Product | Total Sales |
|---------|------------|
| 💻 Laptop | Rs. 255,000 |
| 📱 Phone | Rs. 90,000 |

---

## 🎯 Key Takeaways
- Electronics drives **83% of total revenue**
- Lahore is the **top performing city**
- January peak suggests **seasonal demand** — stock up early!
- April needs **urgent marketing attention**
